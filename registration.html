Registration

Overview and Registration Timeline

Registration for HCCRI 2023 will commence on 15 March 2023. Detailed information, such as the registration timeline and prices, can be found below.

We regret to inform you that HCCRI 2023 will not accept independent applications. All applications should be done through a school teacher or a faculty advisor. If you are a teacher or a faculty advisor but have yet to receive any invitation, do feel free to email us at hccrioperations@gmail.com. Do note that each school is only allowed to invite a maximum of 10 delegates.

Registration Prices

Registration Period Price
Early Bird Registration (15 March - 5 April) $25
Normal Registration (6 April - 27 April) $35

For early bird payment, payment has to be made by 5 April 2023, 2359. For payments after 5 April, normal registration rates will be applied.

Delegate allocations will only be released once full payment has been received. More information regarding the payment method can be found in the invitation email sent to school teachers and faculty advisors.
